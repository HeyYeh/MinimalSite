Music Video — Sad Happy by Circa Waves

A song called Sad Happy for an album called Sad Happy needs a video that embodies the very modern duality of being both Happy and Sad at once.

The natural answer to this is, of course, clowns going through rehab for not being happy enough anymore.

My role?

I was with this project as co-director from the very beginning.

The process for this one was pretty typical of music video pitches: First, I wrote up the initial treatment and my co-director and I presented the idea to the band and label. Then, when we had sign off, I helped produce and creatively direct the video through constant conversations with actors and the art department. Next comes the shoot and post-production, both of which involved me asking people to do things until this video popped into existence. Magical.

We filmed it in an abandoned hospital in Hertfordshire.

My co-director was both scared of abandoned places, and clowns.

It was quite the shoot.
