Music video — Active by Macky Gee

This was a fun one, and the first and only time I've made a music video for a track without lyrics. No, I don't count chanting 'work it' as lyrics.

My role?

I co-directed this entire thing. I wrote the pitch, co-ordinated pre- and post-production, and was telling people what to do on set too.

Oh, and I also made the stripper trousers that feature in the video because finding tear away suit trousers was not trivial.

Here’s a fun fact: if you’re reckless in testing stripper trousers in pre-production, friction burns can happen. Here’s another fun fact: ointment can only help friction burns so much.
