Starburst — Juicy School spec ads

One of a three part spec video campaign for Starburst to place their brand tag of “Unexplainably Juicy” in the school environment for Back To School. The brand is famous for its surreal adverts, so of course I had to keep that trend going…

Insider secret: we used a paddling pool to catch all the juice. We did not paddle in that pool afterwards.

My role?

I co-wrote, directed, and built all of the practical effects for these videos. I also aggresively tried to get someone from Starburst to watch them, which they did and dubbed them... "amazing".

That felt good.
