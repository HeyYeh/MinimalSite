CEEFAX: In the Time of Plague

Whenever I get some spare time, I like to play with game development. I'm no artist or coder or anything like that, but it is a really fun medium for narrative design.

This game was made as part of a game jam — a game-making competition — where the theme was 'Plague'. It ultimately placed second, but had a long life after the competition where it was featured on various indie dev themed online blogs and magazines.

Nostalgia is a powerful thing, and the narrative frame of the real life CEEFAX (God bless its soul) really elevated the dystopian setting.
