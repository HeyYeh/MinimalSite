Skywind cinematics

I'm on the writing team for the gigantic Skyrim mod Skywind, working to recreate and enhance Morrowind in Skyrim's engine.

In the team’s efforts to remake The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind bigger and better, we wanted to upgrade the “Disturbing Dreams” from the original game into full cutscenes.

My role?

I'm the lead writer on the project, and the overall cinematics project creative lead.

I wrote five major dream cinematics, six minor cinematics, and the intro to the entire game. All of this has been illustrated by a very talented comic book artist and we're currently in the process of animating them into the final cinematics.
