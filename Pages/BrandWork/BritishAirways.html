Social Media Awareness - British Airways

British Airways had some bad press regarding their staff’s usage of social media. They were holding a seminar to make staff more aware of social media risks, but needed an irreverent and fun video to avoid the event feeling like they’re telling their staff off.

My role?

I was hired to write this video by a production company I have strong ties with and I was so thrilled.

We were following BA’s centenary celebration, so I wanted to play on their brand history as a British icon by writing in the style of another staple of our culture: Monty Python.

Despite being played to a very large room of uninterested social media abusers, it went down very well indeed!

Legend has it that this video is now hosted on British Airways' internal network on request from their staff, but that can never be known for sure... Unless I ask someone at BA. I should really ask someone...
