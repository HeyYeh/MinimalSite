Print ad — Admix

This ad appeared in the print magazine associated with a few PocketGamer.biz events — the largest media outlet for the business of mobile games.

One of my main roles at Admix has been helping to establish a brand new category of monetisation called In-Play. I've been working towards that aim through PR pushes like this one that normalise the term among industry veterans.

This ad was made to make it clear what Admix does, to create intrigue about the category, and to establish the brand as bold pioneers in this new space. I did that last one by using the word "pioneer"...

Ghost writing social media thought leadership
