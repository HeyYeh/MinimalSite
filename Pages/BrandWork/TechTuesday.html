Tech Tuesdays compilation — Currys PC World

This was a long-running series of 30 second videos that lived on Currys' Twitter. Each one was a bitesize, tongue-in-cheek demonstration of an amazing tech feature from their product lineup.

Fun fact, the blank void that these videos are based in is called "The White World". It started as a practicality — shooting in an infinity studio near our offices was super cheap — but it actually became a major staple of the brand as a whole. It even reached their out of home and TV spots!

My role?

I wrote and creatively directed these, which is a fancy way of saying that I had a lot of brainstorms with the actual directors.

It was always fun to tackle one of these briefs. The tech feature was often something important and practical, but not something that easily played into memorable little videos. Currys was happy to get fun and absurd with it and I leaned into that.

Another fun fact, the colleagues in these videos aren't actors at all. They're actual staff at actual Currys stores in and around London. Amazing.
